About us

Phoenix Energy, located in Dalby, Queensland, delivers reliable electrical design, construction, and maintenance for commercial and industrial projects across the Southern and Western Downs. We handle everything from lighting and power systems to emergency solutions, prioritizing safety, compliance, and efficiency. Our team works closely with clients from start to finish, ensuring every project meets the highest standards.
